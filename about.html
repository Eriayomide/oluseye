Who I Am

I'm a Data Scientist and AI Engineer with over five years of hands-on experience in Python, machine learning, SQL, and data analysis. My core work — building predictive models, designing data pipelines, forecasting, and turning complex datasets into actionable insight — is entirely my own.

My experience spans finance, government platforms, insurance, and social impact.
