Featured Project

Customer Churn Modeling

Customer churn, also referred to as customer attrition or customer defection, describes the situation when customers terminate their relationship with a business or stop using its products or services. The primary objective of this project was to predict potential churn customers using historical data provided by a client through a supervised machine learning approach.

Using SQL queries, data exploration, feature engineering, Variance Inflation Factor, Recursive Feature Elimination, and multicollinearity checks, I reduced 158 derived features to the eight most important predictors. The final predictive model identified over 4,000 potential churners, with the potential to generate significant annual revenue for the business.

HopeBridge AI – RAG Resilience Support System

HopeBridge AI is a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) powered resilience support system designed for Nigerians experiencing life challenges. The platform combines empathetic conversational design, Nigerian-context story retrieval, distress detection, and verified support resources to provide emotionally aware guidance.

The system supports English and Nigerian Pidgin, with planned delivery across web, mobile, and WhatsApp platforms. It uses a structured conversation flow built around four layers: Hear, Witness, Guide, and Connect. HopeBridge AI also includes a three-level safety system for distress detection and escalation to real human support where necessary.

Product Recommendation Engine in Python

This recommendation engine was built in Python to analyze user behavior and generate personalized product suggestions. It uses a collaborative filtering approach with implicit feedback, where historical customer-product interactions are used instead of explicit ratings.

The project involved data preparation, cleaning, sparse matrix construction, and training an ALS model. The final recommender system achieved an AUC score of 77.5%, outperforming the popularity benchmark and demonstrating strong performance in predicting the next best product for customers.
